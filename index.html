Bio

Childish Gambino is the stage name of American actor, comedian, director and producer, Donald McKinley Glover Jr. Born September 25, 1983, the performer has previosuly written for NBC's 30 Rock and played a role in the NBC sitcom Community.

He currently acts on the hit show Atlanta, for which he has won two Primetime Emmy Awards.
